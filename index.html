Daily Health Tips

💧 Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration prevents UTIs and kidney stones.
🌙 Sleep Well
Adults need 7–9 hours of quality sleep. Poor sleep weakens immunity and raises blood pressure.
🥦 Eat Balanced
Include vegetables, fruits, lean protein, and whole grains in every meal for optimal health.
🚶 Move Daily
30 minutes of brisk walking reduces risk of heart disease, diabetes, and depression.
🧴 Skin Care
Use SPF 30+ sunscreen daily and moisturize regularly to protect and maintain healthy skin.
