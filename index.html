About Me

MBBS, FCPS Part-I (Urology), MRCS Part A (England)

I provide consultation and treatment for a wide range of common health conditions affecting both children and adults, including sexual health concerns, fever, cough and cold, diabetes, hypertension, and common skin disorders.

I have a special interest in the evaluation and management of urinary problems, kidney stones, erectile dysfunction, prostate-related conditions, and various men's health issues.

I believe in listening carefully to my patients, understanding their concerns, and providing appropriate medical advice and treatment. When necessary, I recommend relevant investigations and offer clear guidance regarding diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care.

My goal is to deliver high-quality, patient-centered healthcare while maintaining professionalism, confidentiality, compassion, and respect for every patient.

  • Erectile dysfunction assessment and treatment
  • Prostate disease evaluation and management

Daily Health Tips

💧 Stay HydratedDrink at least 8 glasses of water daily to prevent UTIs and kidney stones.
🌙 Sleep WellAdults need 7–9 hours of quality sleep. Poor sleep weakens immunity and raises blood pressure.
🥦 Eat BalancedInclude vegetables, fruits, lean protein, and whole grains in every meal.
🚶 Move Daily30 minutes of brisk walking daily reduces risk of heart disease, diabetes, and depression.
🧴 Skin CareUse SPF 30+ sunscreen daily and moisturize regularly to protect your skin.
