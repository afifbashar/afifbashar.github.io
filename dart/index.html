Argentina Wins World Cup 2022

Lionel Messi finally achieved his dream as Argentina defeated France in a thrilling final. The match ended 3-3 after extra time and Argentina won 4-2 on penalties. Kylian Mbappé scored a brilliant hat-trick, but goalkeeper Emiliano Martínez became the hero by saving two penalties.

Millions of fans celebrated across Argentina. This victory gave them their third World Cup title after 36 years.
