Argentina Wins World Cup 2022 in Dramatic Final

Lionel Messi finally won the World Cup as Argentina beat France in one of the greatest finals ever. The match ended 3-3 after extra time, with Argentina winning 4-2 on penalties. Kylian Mbappé scored a stunning hat-trick, but Emiliano Martínez saved two penalties to give Argentina their third World Cup.

Millions celebrated in Buenos Aires as Messi lifted the trophy he had dreamed of for years.
